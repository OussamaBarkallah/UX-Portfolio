UX Research - Interaction Designer

Lacito : Design Software

Hello, I share with you the development of innovative software for field linguistics, designed to transform how researchers collect, organize, and valorize linguistic data. The tool directly addresses the domain’s major challenges: data collection, inter-researcher collaboration, and the preservation of endangered languages.

Lacito : Design Software

The design approach was intensively user-centered: it included a comprehensive benchmark of existing tools, in-depth interviews, and a full UX Research phase (Journey Map, Personas, Empathy Map, etc.). This rigorous process resulted in a software solution that streamlines and systematizes the organization and archiving of linguistic data, establishing an essential platform for the sustainability of minority languages.
