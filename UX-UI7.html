UX/UI designer

Coaching Pro

As part of a team, I helped design a website for Coaching Pro, a platform that supports recent graduates and professionals looking to change careers in finding their ideal jobs. The platform provides a secure space where users can share documents and ideas, book live online coaching sessions (individual or group), and access training programs led by professional coaches.

Coaching Pro

We used the Design Sprint method — a five-day iterative process — to create an engaging and safe user experience. The final website allows users to book sessions easily, track their training, and prepare effectively for interviews.​​​​​​​
