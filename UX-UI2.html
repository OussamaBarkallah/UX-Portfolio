Mobile App USPN Students

Project Overview

This project was developed during a team-based hackathon and focuses on designing a mobile application to improve student communication and access to university services at Université Sorbonne Paris Nord.

Problem

Students at Université Sorbonne Paris Nord struggled to access essential information. Schedules, academic emails, and university services were scattered across multiple platforms, leading to missed updates and confusion.

Solution

We designed a centralized mobile application that brings schedules, emails, and key university services into one intuitive interface, with real-time notifications for important updates.

Impact

  • Improved information clarity and accessibility
  • Faster access to university services
  • Reduced missed emails and schedule changes
  • Better student engagement through notifications

Product Overview

The product features a clear and intuitive interface that allows students to easily view their class schedules and university emails, book medical appointments, join sports sessions, and stay informed about campus activities. Personalized notifications alert users to important updates such as schedule changes, professor absences, or new messages ensuring that every student stays connected and up to date.
