UX Research - UI design

Add features: Basic Fit

Hi all! We’re thrilled to share our UX enhancement project for Basic Fit, one of Europe’s leading fitness brands. Working collaboratively as a team, we focused on improving the app experience by introducing new features that highlight the brand’s growing impact in the fitness industry.

Add features: Basic Fit

To better understand user expectations, we adopted a user-centered design approach combining qualitative and quantitative research. We identified key user needs, conducted interviews and surveys with gym-goers, and analyzed the collected data to uncover meaningful insights. Based on our findings, we designed and prototyped a new app section allowing users to visualize the nationwide Basic Fit network and book workout sessions effortlessly—turning data into an engaging and interactive brand experience.
